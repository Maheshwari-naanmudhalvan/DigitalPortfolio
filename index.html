Tribute TO Maruthu Pandiyar

About Maruthu Pandiyar

The Marudhu Pandiyars[1] (Periya Marudhu and Chinna Marudhu) were de facto rulers of Sivagangai, Tamil Nadu, India, towards the end of the 18th century. They were known for fighting against the East India Company.[2] They were finally executed by the EIC after being captured by them.
